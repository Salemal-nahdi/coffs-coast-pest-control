Cockroach Specialisation & Control

Cockroaches are a persistent issue across our region, thriving in coastal warmth and sub-floor dampness. From minor kitchen nuisances to heavy German cockroach infestations, I use precise, target-specific baiting and barrier systems to clear them out safely and permanently.

Signs You Need This Service

Identifying a pest problem early can save thousands in structural repairs and protect your family's health. Watch out for these typical indicators on your property:

  • Small, pepper-like droppings located in hinges, cupboard corners, or behind whitegoods.
  • Unexplained egg casings (oothecae) appearing in dark drawers or pantry crevices.
  • Activity during daylight hours, which usually indicates an established, hidden colony.

I believe in transparent, straightforward service. When I come out to inspect or treat your property, here is the exact practical checklist we will cover:

1

Thermal Harborage Scan
