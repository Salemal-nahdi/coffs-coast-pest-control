Practical Advice & FAQs

Straightforward answers about our treatments, safety, and recommendations.

Are the pest treatments safe for family and pets?

Absolutely. I select premium-grade, low-toxicity products that are strictly registered for use in schools, homes, and hospitals. Once the sprayed barriers have dried completely (typically about 1 to 2 hours), it is entirely safe for kids, dogs, cats, and birds to return to their normal routines.

Why do we need termite checks every 12 months?

Under Australian Standard AS 3660, regular professional termite inspections are strongly recommended at least annually. Coastal NSW features high humidity and moisture levels, which create the absolute perfect breeding ground for destructive subterranean termites. An annual inspection detects early signs before they cause thousands of dollars in timber frame damage.

How should I prepare my property before a treatment?

You don't need to do any major heavy lifting. Just clear small items and toys off the floors, pull furniture slightly away from internal skirting boards if easily moved, cover any pet water bowls, and make sure washing is off the outdoor clothesline. I'll handle all the professional setups from there.
