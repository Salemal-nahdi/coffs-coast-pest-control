Common Grafton Pest Pressures

The Clarence Valley climate and historic timber architecture require specialized defense strategies. Here is what we regularly protect properties against in Grafton:

  • Termite Risks: High subterranean termite pressure, particularly threatening Grafton's older, heritage-listed timber homes.
  • Riverine Pests: Mosquitoes, midges, and spiders thrive due to the proximity of the Clarence River and surrounding floodplains.
  • Cockroaches & Ants: Prevalent throughout residential blocks during the humid Northern Rivers summers.
